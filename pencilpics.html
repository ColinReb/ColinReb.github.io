Readme

This app converts any image from your gallery or disk or camera into a "Sketch".

Note: When starting the app for the first time, a popup may appear asking for your permission to use the camera. This is pretty standard for any browser app. You will need to accept this on the popup. !
After you click the "Start" button you will go to a page where you can either choose the camera or an image on your disk / gallery. You can adjust the image settings using the sliders on the page.
This app is free. For privacy reasons none of the images are ever transmitted to any server ! All the image manipulations happen inside the browser only.
